Sleep Without Barriers

FeatherPad is an innovative sleeping pad that will allow you to sleep with your baby with peace of mind. By implementing smart technology to track your baby and its surroundings, we make sure that you and your baby can sleep together in comfort and safety throughout the night.

Co-sleeping is defined as the practice of a parent sleeping in the same bed as their infant child. This can prove beneficial in the baby's upbringing and offer a close connection between the baby and parent. Yet many co-sleepers today tend to be obtrusive and put barriers between the baby and the parent. FeatherPad aims to deliver a non-intrusive, yet safe product that will fulfill your desire to have a nurturing relationship with your baby even while you sleep!
