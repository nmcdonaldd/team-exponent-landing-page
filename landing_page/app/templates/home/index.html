Sleep Without Barriers

What is FeatherPad?

FeatherPad is an innovative sleeping pad that will allow you to sleep with your baby with peace of mind. By implementing smart technology to track your baby and its surroundings, we can make sure you and your baby can sleep together in comfort.

Why FeatherPad?

Co-sleeping is defined as the practice of sleeping in the same bed with one's infant or young child. Yet many co-sleepers out there distance your baby away from you. FeatherPad aims to deliver a non-intrusive, yet safe product that will fulfill your desire to have a nurturing relationship with your baby even while you sleep!
