Cosleeping is the practice of a parent sleeping in the same bed as their infant child

Cosleeping can be scary for parents to try out because of the danger of SIDs, or Sudden Infant Deaths. However, FeatherPad aims to relieve those anxieties by providing a comfy surface for your baby to sleep on while keeping track of your baby’s surroundings to ensure the safety of your child.

Once you open up the FeatherPad, locate your FeatherPad’s unique device id and create an account on our website using this id. Plug in the FeatherPad and lay your baby on it. That’s it! Also install our mobile app on the iOS App Store to make sure you are being alerted when your baby is in an unsafe environment.
