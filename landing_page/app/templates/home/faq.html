Cosleeping is the practice of a parent sleeping in the same bed as their infant child

Here are some advantages to cosleeping:

  • Parents often get more sleep
  • Babies often get more sleep
  • During the night, it is easier to nurse your baby
  • Nighttime separation anxiety isn't a thing anymore
  • You get to see your baby first thing when you wake up!
